##Overview
 - Bill Pay enables Vanteo Connect customers to pay U.S. bills directly from their Vanteo checking account within the mobile app. The solution leverages SPDR's Bill Pay APIs, which integrate with Galileo's Bill Pay platform on behalf of sponsor bank FBOL.

#Problem
 - Customers currently rely on external banks or biller websites to manage recurring payments, creating friction and reducing engagement with Vanteo as their primary banking solution.

#Goal
 - Deliver a secure, intuitive Bill Pay experience that allows customers to discover billers, make one-time and scheduled payments, track payment status, and manage their payments from a single application.

#Business Objectives
Increase primary account usage
Improve customer retention and engagement
Grow recurring payment activity
Expand Vanteo's digital banking capabilities

#V1 Scope
 - Search and add billers and payees
 - Make One-time bill payments
 - Payment history and status
 - View Billers and Payees
 - View upcoming payments due from linked Billers
